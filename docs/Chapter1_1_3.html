The Role of COMS in Mini-grid Management

Purpose

The goal of COMS is to provide a platform for different mini-grid actors to effectively communicate, through standardised workflows, easy-to-use interfaces, third-party hardware interoperability, rapid and automated data aggregation, and transparent and reliable record-keeping.

Functionalities

COMS is built upon two critical design principles:

  1. Accurate and comprehensive record-keeping

    1. Including centralised databases of historical technical and financial data, user information, plant specifications, system changes, etc.

  2. Real-time communication, control, and updates

    1. A responsive system must accurately propagate and distribute commands issued from any node, and provide up-to-date information to all users

COMS is designed to cover all aspects of business monitoring, and crucially places sales and finance metrics alongside technical performance and analysis of trends in customer demand. This synthesis of information gives mini-grid developers a deeper understanding of their projects’ development, and establishes the foundations of a successful and sustainable business.

Users

Mini-grid businesses are multi-faceted, with many different personnel and stakeholder roles. COMS caters to these user groups’ requirements and workflows, as described in the following sections.
